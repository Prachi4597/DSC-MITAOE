Developer Student Club MIT-AOE

What We Do

DSC or Developers Student Club is a program under Google Developers that targets undergraduate students.

It's main focus is to help the students identify the current technologies used in the industry and educate them for the same so that they become excellent developers.

About

DSC is all about development

DSC activities are targeted towards students (primarily University students and any others including faculty members)

who want to learn development [native app & mobile web] skills. As part of the learning experience for students, you can work with local stakeholders who may include local businesses or NGOs or other organizations.
